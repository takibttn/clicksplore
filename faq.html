Account Questions

How do shared accounts work?

We purchase premium subscriptions and securely share access with multiple users. Each member gets their own profile where applicable (like Netflix) or secure login credentials for services that don't support multiple profiles.

Is this legal?

Yes, our service operates within the terms of service of most platforms. We're essentially acting as a family plan manager, which is permitted by most subscription services.

What if my login stops working?

We guarantee access for the duration of your subscription. If you experience any issues, contact our support team for an immediate replacement.
